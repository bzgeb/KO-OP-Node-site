KOLAB Games

INFO

KOLAB is an initiative by KO-OP to fund the development of small games. Gamemakers are given 2000$ CAD to make a game within a one to three month period. The goal is to provide game-makers outside the mainstream a small but significant opportunity to realize the games they want to make. Our hope is that KOLAB inspires other insitutions and models to enrich the ecosystem for #altgames to become more sustainable. KOLAB partners are discovered through a mix of submissions and invitations.

SUBMIT

Submissions are currently open. If you're curious about what kind of things we're looking for, how the fund works, or what the process is like, please read our FAQ below. Each submission will be considered, and you will recieve a confirmation that the subbmision was recieved. Please note, we have no date set for making our decision so please be patient with us.

KO-OP encourages applications from Black and indigenous individuals, people of colour, individuals living with disabilities, transgender people, women, LGBTQ people, and those who identify as genderqueer and/or non-binary.

FAQ

  • How much is the fund and how is it paid out?
    - The fund is 2000$ Canadian and is paid half at the start and half at the completion of the game.
  • What kind of games are you looking for?
    - We're open to almost any kind of game that can be made within the 1-3 month period. If you check out our games you'll see we have a heavy emphasis on visuals but we encourage all forms including things like twine based games.
  • Do I own the game I make?
    - Absolutely. You own everything that you create, and you license it to KO-OP so that we can talk about it, post it on our games list, and otherwise help spread the word.
  • What's your cut from the game's sales?
    - That's up to you. If you feel like splitting a percentage with KO-OP we would gladly accept, but if making this game and its income are critical to your survival then we are okay giving up any right to collect money. All we really require is a KO-OP/KOLAB credit.
  • So what are you getting out of this?
    - Two things: the first is that we're hoping that this inspires more groups to put money behind these sorts of games. The second would be that this helps KO-OP spread its name and ideas (basically if your game is popular then KO-OP gets a bit more exposure).
  • Can I make a game to be released for free?
    - Yes.
  • How many projects are you going to fund?
    - Right now we're committing to funding at least one project during 2015.
  • Can I submit a project already in development?
    - It depends. If you thin the project will be completed within 1-3 months then sure. Our ideal candidate is making a small game that can be made from start to finish within the 1-3 month period however.
  • I have more questions not answered here
    - No problem, shoot us an email
