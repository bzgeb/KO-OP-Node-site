KO_OP is an artist run and owned studio. Founded in 2012, we've been experiminting with games and interactive art ever since. In 2014 we established the KOLAB fund, a small stipend for outside artists to make games with our help.

WORK FOR HIRE

KO_OP takes on contract work from time to time. For business inquiries contact us.

Cancer of Time — KO_OP was in charge of art direction and programming. Game design was done in collaboration with the team from the National Film Board and FranceTV.

RigBMX 2 Crash Curse — made for Cartoon Network as a follow up to the popular RigBMX. KO_OP was in charge of all aspects of the game from design to cross-browser HTML5 development.

Biocube — is a meditative experience produced by the National Film Board. KO_OP was tasked with designing and developing a VR prototype that invovled hand gesture tracking with the Leap Motion.

Museum of Symmetry — A prototype for Paloma Dawkins's new game, produced by the National Film Board. KO_OP was tasked with creating an early vertical slice of one of the game's biomes.

RigBMX — a popular web game developed for Cartoon Network. KO_OP was in charge of all aspects of the game from design to cross-browser HTML5 development.

Dad by the Sword — Currently in development, KO_OP has developed the game's art direction and programming.
