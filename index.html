About Us

KO-OP Mode is a collective of independent game developers out of Montréal, Québec. Inspired by the example of indie record labels, KO-OP brings together designers of all stripes and sensibilities in one place (presently: a bedroom) to jam on both personal projects and group collaborations, with an emphasis on experimentation in design, aesthetics, and narrative.
